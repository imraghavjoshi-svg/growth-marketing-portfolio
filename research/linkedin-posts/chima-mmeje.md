[My top SEO tips for 2026 are...


1. Build entity clusters that cover the full query fan-out

We published research showing that LLMs like Google's AI mode don't rely on a single search query when generating answers.

Instead, they run multiple related queries to build answers from a broader intent set.

Topic clusters help you cover the full intent for the query while satisfying intent on a granular level.

2. Evolve reporting beyond traffic

We need to change reporting as clicks decline. There are a bunch of things you can track to show results like:

LLM referrals
Self-attribution
Brand demand as an influence proxy
LLM citation depth
Session quality signals

3. Use community as a growth engine

For as long as humans exist on earth, community will remain a growth lever to:

Strengthen bonds with customers
Understand language your target audience uses when talking about your product
Collect feedback
Support product launches

And so many more things you can do with community.

4. Optimize for Agentic AI and autonomous search

I hate to say this, but it's happening... agentic browsing will be part of the future of search and conversion. This is where having GEO expertise as a technical SEO really shines through.

The most important thing is to fix crawl frictions that would hurt conversion paths to ensure bots and agents can crawl your pages and take action.


More tips in my Whiteboard Friday that's packed full of actionable advice for SEO & content teams

Let me know which tip you resonated with when you're done reading. ]
